DuneSurfer

DuneSurfer creates Psydance — hypnotic electronic music built from desert silence, euphoric pressure, tribal rhythm and cosmic sound design. The sound is physical, cinematic and made for late-night movement.

Psydance lives where psytrance, trance and EDM collide — hypnotic basslines, euphoric builds, dancefloor energy, desert mysticism and cosmic atmosphere fused into an original genre world.

What is Psydance?

Psydance is where psytrance, trance and EDM dance music meet. Hypnotic basslines from psytrance, euphoric builds from trance, and the raw dancefloor energy of EDM — fused with desert heat, cosmic depth and psychedelic precision. DuneSurfer created it. The dancefloor defines it.
