DuneSurfer Portal Comparison

Three 3D portal options — hover each to interact

Option 1: Wormhole Tunnel

A deep tunnel receding into space with ring gates, spiraling energy, and streaking star particles. You look into it from an offset angle.

Option 2: Particle Vortex Sphere

3000 particles swirling in a sphere/torus shape with orbital frequency rings, a pulsing core, and spark trails. The whole structure rotates.

Option 3: Portal Disc with Depth

18 layered transparent discs stacked in depth — spirals, ripples, and nebula — with parallax on mouse move and floating motes around the edges.

Move your mouse over each portal to see parallax/interaction. All respond to audio when playing.
