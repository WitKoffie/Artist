Artist bio

DuneSurfer

DuneSurfer is a psytrance producer channelling desert mysticism through cosmic frequencies. The sound blends hypnotic basslines, psychedelic textures, tribal rhythms and vast atmospheric landscapes into an experience that transcends the dancefloor.

Drawing inspiration from the silence of desert expanses and the infinite depth of the cosmos, DuneSurfer creates music that is both primal and futuristic. Every track is a journey through inner and outer space.

Building a sound that sits at the intersection of progressive psytrance, dark psychedelic and full-on energy.

What is DuneSurfer?

DuneSurfer is where desert mysticism meets cosmic psytrance. It is not a genre label, it is a frequency. Hypnotic basslines built from ancient rhythms, psychedelic textures layered with cosmic atmospheres, and driving energy designed to dissolve the boundary between dancer and universe. Born from silence. Built for transcendence.
