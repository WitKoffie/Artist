Artist bio

WitKoffie

WitKoffie is a South African electronic music producer creating AfriHuis. Afrikaans House Music rebuilt for the future. Rooted in Afrikaans emotion, shaped by South African rhythm, and built for modern speakers.

The sound blends deep house warmth, kwaito movement, and Afrikaans vocal texture into something that feels both familiar and new. Every track is brewed with intention. Warm bass, clean production, and lyrics that resonate.

With 8 released singles and a growing catalogue, WitKoffie is building a genre, not following one.

What is AfriHuis?

AfriHuis is Afrikaans House Music. Not nostalgia, not a remix of the past. It is Afrikaans rhythm rebuilt for modern speakers. Deep house warmth meets kwaito energy, layered with Afrikaans vocal texture and South African soul. Gebrou in Afrikaans. Geskink vir die wêreld.
